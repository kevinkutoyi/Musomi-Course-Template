CIM 897: COMPETENCY BASED CURRICULUM

Audience Description

This course is offered to students pursuing Masters of Education in Educational Communication & Technology (Pedagogy) and Master of Education in Curriculum Studies. Post graduate students taking other programmes in the Department of Curriculum, Instruction & Educational Media and School of Education can take the course as an elective.

    iii)   Degree level: Masters

    iv)   Year of Study: First year

    v)    Semester: Second Semester

About this Course

Welcome to the course CIM 897: Competency Based Curriculum. This course is designed to provide you with knowledge, skills and attitudes on Competency Based Curriculum. The core concepts of the course include the philosophy and theories that inform the design of Competency Based Curriculum; principles and core competences for basic education in Kenya; Competency Based Assessment practices; teacher capacity development requirements for implementation of Competence Based Curriculum; challenges, global practices and research on Competency Based Education.

 

Active participation and cooperation, timely submission of assignments, and collaboration with peers are essential for successful completion of the course. This course contains 10 Modules to be covered in 10 weeks with interactive activities that enhance your progress. The assignments and activities will contribute towards 50% of your assessment score and the other 50% will be obtained from end of semester exam. You are expected to complete all the activities in each module by the end of the semester.

Module Objectives

This course will enable you to learn about:

  1. Philosophy and theories that inform the design of Competency Based Curriculum.
  2. Principles and core competences in the structure of basic education in Kenya
  3. Competency Based Assessment practices
  4. Teacher capacity development requirements for implementation of Competence Based Curriculum.
Module Learning Outcomes

By the end of the course, you should be able to:

  1. Describe the philosophy and theories that inform the design of Competency Based Curriculum.
  2. Discuss the principles and core competences in the structure of basic education in Kenya
  3. Appraise Competency Based Assessment practices
  4. Assess teacher capacity development requirements for implementation of Competence Based Curriculum.
